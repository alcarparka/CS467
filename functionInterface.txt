Calculator function Interface:

tpcf(coordinateFileName, maxSpaceX, minSpaceX, maxSpaceY, minSpaceY, maxSpaceZ, minSpaceZ, maxLength, minLength, numBins, randomMultiplier, periodicFlag)

- coordinateFile: either the file containing the coordinates to be read in by the function, or the coordinates themselves in a list.

- maxSpaceN: the maximum possible coordinate of the space in the N dimension

- minSpaceN: the minimum possible coordinate of the space in the N dimension

- maxLength: the maximum correlation length to consider

- minLength: the minimum correlation length to consider

- numBins: the number of bins to calculate pair counts over (default to 10)

- randomMultiplier: factor to multiply the count of data coordinates to generate random coordinates (randomCount = randomMultiplier*dataCount) (default to 30)

- periodicFlag: boolean flag indicating whether to use periodic boundary conditions (default to True)
